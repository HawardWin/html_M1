Who ?

Maheshwara, in Hinduism, signifies Lord Shiva, honored for his divine qualities and role as the supreme deity responsible for creation, preservation, and destruction, reflecting the holistic nature of the universe's cycle

Why ?

                From: Mahabharata (English)
                
                The Lord of the universe, often referred to as the supreme deity in Hindu philosophy, encompassing creation, preservation, and destruction.
                                   
                From: Ramayana of Valmiki (Shastri)
